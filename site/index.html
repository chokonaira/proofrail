Live browser sandbox · real proofs, simulated side effects

Proof-gated actions for AI agents

PermitRail sits between an agent and a risky tool call. It checks policy, asks for approval of the exact request, signs a short-lived proof, lets the action run once, and writes a receipt you can verify later.

What's real here

Real cryptography, in this tab

Ed25519 keys are generated and every proof and receipt is signed by @permitrail/core in your browser. Hit Verify to check a signature, or Tamper to watch it fail.

Faked side effects only

The email, payment, and database tools return canned JSON. No request leaves your browser. Nothing is sent, charged, or deleted.
